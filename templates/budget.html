💡 Money-Saving Tips

✈️ Flights

  • Book 6–8 weeks in advance
  • Fly mid-week (Tue/Wed)
  • Use incognito mode to search
  • Compare 3–5 booking sites

🏨 Accommodation

  • Stay outside city centre
  • Book refundable rates
  • Consider hostels or homestays
  • Look for last-minute deals

🍽️ Food

  • Eat where locals eat
  • Street food is safe & cheap
  • Get breakfast-included stays
  • Visit local markets

🚇 Transport

  • Use public transport passes
  • Walk or bike when possible
  • Book trains early online
  • Consider overnight buses
